What is Devoir?

Devoir provides a CDN (Content Delivery Network) solution. Our unique feature that distinguishes us from competitors is delivering content to visitors faster, while saving money.

To do that, Devoir uses Google Analytics of a website, and optimizes the location of servers and bandwith usage. Giving an optimal price/solution automatically.
